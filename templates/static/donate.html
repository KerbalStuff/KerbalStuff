Donate


As you might know Spacedock only survives with your help.

Apart from donating money to keep the Infrastructure running you can also donate time or hardware:

Donate Money

You can donate money via Patreon or wire it to VITASs Bank account.

Donate Time

We are always shorthanded.
To prevent us from burning out we are looking for every Team-player whos willing to help.
We offer a professional Project with lots of opertunities to get involved.
We are looking for:
  • Network Admins / System Engineers
    (Linux, Docker, MariaDB, Apache,...)
    to help us with our infrastructure
    (We are running our own global CDN)
  • Coders
    (Mainly Python but PHP doesn't hurt)
    to help with backend code
  • More Coders
    (c#)
    to code new exciting things
  • Frontend Devs
    (HTML5, JS, CSS3,..the usual)
    to help improving and bugfixing our UI.
  • Graphic artists
    (Photoshop, Illustrator, 3D Software)
    Theres always the need to make graphics...
  • Nice Humans
    (We use OTRS for Support Mails)
    to help with support.


Hardware

You can either donate Servers if they conform to the following specs:
  • In a propper Datacenter
  • Can provide root access (at least to a vm)
  • No Atom or ARM CPU
  • At least 4GB of RAM
  • At least 100 Mbit/s Connection
  • Available for at least 6 month
  • At least 160GB of usable Diskspace
OR you can send spare Hardware to our Team members to give them a great tools to produce great things for you.
(We are spread out all around the globe so there should be someone near you in despereate need for some spare parts)

Tell others
