Terms of Use

We can and will ban you from the site if you fail to follow these terms. We may also take legal action against more serious violations.

The general idea is "be responsible". Follow these baisc rules:

  • Do not upload illegal content to the site
  • Do not abuse or harrass other users
  • Upload only content you can claim copyright to
  • Uploaders offer Kerbal Stuff a royalty-free, worldwide license to distribute their mods upon uploading them

Privacy Policy

Kerbal Stuff makes an effort to preserve your privacy, while still giving the administration the ability to issue bans and take action against abuse. With that in mind, the only thing we keep around that you cannot opt-out of is your IP address (in addition to the account information you may voluntarily enter on the site).

Data Collection

Kerbal Stuff uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous data on your use of the site, and aggregates it with the data from other users. This data includes (but is not limited to):

  • Your country of origin
  • Your browser and operating sytem
  • The website you used to get here (i.e. Reddit, Google, etc)
  • Your preferred language
  • Your Internet Service Provider
  • The amount of time you spent on the site and which pages you were on

You are able to opt-out of this tracking by setting the Do Not Track flag on your browser.

Removing Content

You are able to delete mods you upload to Kerbal Stuff. When you do so, all traces of your mods are deleted from the service and are unrecoverable.

If you find your content on the site and you did not upload it or do not want it to be there, please email support to explain your case.
