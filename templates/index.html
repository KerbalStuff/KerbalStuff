Kerbal Stuff is a community modding site

There have been a few poor modding sites for Kerbal Space Program in the past. Kerbal Stuff aims to be different. We fix the problems with the old and make the new work great. Most of all, Kerbal Stuff brings the Kerbal Space Program community back into the loop and puts the power in the modder's hands.

Great for Players

Get email updates

Easily keep track of your favorite mods. Don't bother checking the forums every 5 minutes, just sit back and we'll email you when the time comes.

Easy to use search

Searching for mods on Kerbal Stuff is painless and easy. You'll find the mod you're looking for on the first try, or your money back.

Full version history

Got a nostalgic itch to scratch? Kerbal Stuff keeps around old versions of mods and includes the last version of KSP they worked with.

Find new and cool mods

We don't need to tell you about the popular mods you already know about. Instead, we feature new and interesting mods to give you something new.

Great for Modders

Get detailed stats

We'll give you a history of downloads and we'll give it to you with nice pretty graphs. You can also export the raw data if you want to visualize it yourself.

Update in moments

No nonsense updates. Give us a version number, a zip file, and optionally a changelog, and your followers get an email with the latest version.

Exposure where it's due

Got a popular mod? It'll be the first in the search results. Got a cool brand-new mod? We'll make sure you get the exposure you need.

Open source

Wouldn't it be great if you could help make your modding site even better? You can! Kerbal Stuff is open source, come check out the code.

Register

Ready to make an account? You'll be able to:

  • Upload new mods
  • Keep track of your favorite mods
  • Tell your friends "I have a Kerbal Stuff account!"
  • Replace the "Log In" link with a "Log Out" link
  • And not much more!
